# Wiki Entry Template

## Overview
This document provides a standardized template to use when beggining your wiki contribution. 
Copy the text below and paste it into the file you want to commit as your wiki contribution.
Any text within square brackets [  ] is a section for you to enter text for your document.
Text labeled with a * is optional or situational.
[del] text is used to keep formatting, but should be removed when pasted into your document.

---
tags:

 - [...]
---
#[title]

##[Subtitle_1]
[text of entry for subtitle 1]

```
````
